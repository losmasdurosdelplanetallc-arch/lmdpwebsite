24.2MP APS-C / 4K video / 425 phase-detect AF

Best for vlogs, reels, talking-head content, product shots, creator videos, and fast autofocus scenes where the subject needs to stay sharp while moving.

33MP full-frame / 4K 60p 10-bit / 759 phase-detect AF

Best for high-end music videos, cinematic portraits, low-light scenes, brand campaigns, and projects that need richer detail, color, and a more premium visual feel.

Neumann TLM 102

Cardioid condenser / 20 Hz-20 kHz / 144 dB SPL

Best for premium lead vocals, detailed rap and singing takes, ad-libs, voiceovers, and sessions where the vocal needs to feel crisp, present, and release-ready.

RODE Wireless GO II

Dual-channel wireless / 2.4 GHz / up to 200 m range

Best for interviews, vlogs, event coverage, BTS, tutorials, and talking-head content where people need to move freely while the audio stays clear.

Apollo Twin USB

10 x 6 USB 3 / 24-bit 192 kHz / UAD-2 DUO DSP

Yamaha HS5

5-inch woofer / 54 Hz-30 kHz / 70 W bi-amp

The Crew

LMDP is a small, hands-on creative crew built by people who live the artist process from the inside. We understand the pressure of making something feel real, because we are creators too.
